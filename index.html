Your Ritual

How To Use

A simple, effective routine for radiant skin. Follow these steps morning and night.

01

Cleanse

Start with a gentle cleanser to remove impurities. Pat skin dry — never rub.

02

Tone

Apply toner to balance pH and prepare skin to absorb serums more effectively.

03

Apply Serum

Dispense 2–3 drops of your Verdena serum. Gently press into skin with upward motions.

04

Moisturize

Lock everything in with a moisturizer suited to your skin type.

05

Protect

In the morning, finish with SPF 30+. Sun protection is the most effective anti-aging step.
